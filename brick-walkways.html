Brick walkways are classic hardscape features that never go out of style. They provide defined pathways through your landscape, connect different areas of your property, and add architectural interest that complements both traditional and contemporary home designs. With proper installation, brick walkways can last for decades while requiring minimal maintenance.

Brick Options & Design Patterns

Clay Bricks

Traditional clay bricks available in red, brown, and tan. Classic durability with a timeless aesthetic.

Concrete Pavers

Consistent size and multiple colour options. Very cost-effective with excellent durability for our climate.

Tumbled Bricks

Aged appearance with rounded edges for a more rustic, old-world aesthetic that suits cottage and heritage properties.

Reclaimed Bricks

Vintage character and unique patina, with each brick being one of a kind. Perfect for properties with historic character.

Popular Laying Patterns

Running Bond is a classic, linear pattern for a clean traditional look. Herringbone is an elegant interlocking design with excellent stability. Basketweave offers a timeless woven appearance. Jack-on-Jack is a simple grid pattern for a bold, contemporary feel.

Our Installation Process

1

Layout & Excavation

We mark out the pathway, excavate to the correct depth, and establish proper grades for drainage away from your home and structures.

2

Base Preparation

A compacted gravel base is installed to the proper depth. This is the most critical step for preventing settling and shifting over time.

3

Sand Bed & Edge Restraints

A precisely screeded sand leveling course is laid, and edge restraints are set to hold the perimeter bricks permanently in place.

4

Brick Laying & Finishing

Bricks are laid in your chosen pattern, cut for perfect edges, then compacted and finished with polymeric joint sand for a long-lasting result.
