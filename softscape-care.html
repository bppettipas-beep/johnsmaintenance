Softscape care involves the maintenance and health of all the living elements in your landscape, including your grass, plants, trees, and soil. Our comprehensive approach ensures your outdoor space remains lush, healthy, and beautiful throughout the growing season. With over 40 years of local experience, we understand exactly what New Brunswick landscapes need to thrive through our climate.

Garden Bed Upkeep & Weeding

  • Regular weeding to keep beds clean and tidy
  • Soil loosening and bed maintenance
  • Seasonal cleanup of dead growth
  • Plant health monitoring

Mulching & Edging

  • Fresh mulch installation and top-ups
  • Clean bed edge definition
  • Moisture retention and weed suppression
  • Improved overall appearance

Shrub Planting, Trimming & Shaping

  • New shrub selection and installation
  • Precision trimming and shaping
  • Overgrowth removal and thinning
  • Seasonal pruning for healthy growth

Garden Refresh & Curb Appeal Upgrades

  • Full garden bed redesigns and replanting
  • New plantings for colour and texture
  • Front yard transformations
  • Seasonal refresh for lasting impressions
