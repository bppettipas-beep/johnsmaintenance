Retaining walls are essential hardscape features that serve both functional and aesthetic purposes. They prevent soil erosion, create level planting areas, define landscape spaces, and can dramatically increase your property's usable outdoor area while adding significant value to your home.

Material Options for Every Style & Budget

Segmented Concrete Blocks

Best for: Residential applications, straight runs

Cost-effective with a consistent appearance. Available in smooth, textured, and split-face finishes with multiple colour options.

Natural Stone

Best for: Premium installations, curved designs

Timeless beauty with unique natural character. Fieldstone, limestone, and granite options offer lasting visual appeal.

Timber Walls

Best for: Garden beds, smaller walls

Natural appearance at a cost-effective price point. Available in pressure-treated lumber or cedar.

Specialty Systems

Best for: Unique situations

Boulder walls, gabion systems, and engineered precast solutions for situations that require specialty approaches.

Why Proper Construction Matters

A Wall Is Only as Good as Its Foundation

The most common reason retaining walls fail is inadequate foundation preparation and poor drainage. Our experienced team builds every wall to last, starting from the ground up.

Structural IntegrityWhat We Ensure

  • Proper foundation depth and width
  • Adequate base preparation
  • Correct setback and batter angle
  • Quality backfill materials
  • Proper compaction techniques

Drainage SystemsPreventing Failure

  • French drain installation
  • Weep holes for water release
  • Proper backfill grading
  • Geotextile fabric installation
  • Surface water management
