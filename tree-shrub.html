Proper Planting Techniques

Getting It Right the First Time

Successful tree and shrub establishment depends on proper planting techniques. Our team follows industry best practices to ensure every plant thrives.

1

Site Evaluation & Preparation

We assess soil conditions, sunlight, drainage, and available space to select the right plants and prepare the site properly.

2

Proper Hole Sizing

Holes are dug 2 to 3 times the width of the root ball and at the correct depth to keep the root flare at soil level.

3

Careful Planting

Root inspection, proper backfilling with appropriate soil mix, initial deep watering, and mulching to retain moisture.

4

Staking & Aftercare

Staking when required for stability, plus detailed aftercare instructions and follow-up care recommendations.
