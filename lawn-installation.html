Sod vs. Seed: Which is Right for You?

We'll Help You Choose

Both sod and seed installation have their advantages. We'll help you select the best option based on your budget, timeline, and specific site conditions.

Sod InstallationBenefits

  • Instant lawn appearance
  • Immediate erosion control
  • Weed-free establishment
  • Can be installed spring through fall
  • Perfect for high-traffic areas

Seed InstallationBenefits

  • More cost-effective option
  • Greater variety selection
  • Better root system development
  • Ideal for large areas
  • Custom grass blend options

Best Grass Types for New Brunswick

We select grass varieties that thrive in our Maritime climate, ensuring your new lawn will be resilient and beautiful year after year.

Kentucky Bluegrass

Dense, durable, and excellent for high-traffic areas. A top choice for New Brunswick properties.

Perennial Ryegrass

Quick establishment and good wear tolerance, great for areas that need rapid cover.

Fine Fescue

Drought tolerant and well-suited for shaded areas where other grasses struggle.

Custom Blends

Tailored mixes designed for your property's specific sun, shade, and soil conditions.
