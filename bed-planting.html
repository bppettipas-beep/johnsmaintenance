Design Principles We Follow

Color Harmony

Coordinated color schemes that look beautiful throughout the seasons and complement your home's exterior.

Texture Contrast

Mixing different leaf shapes and plant forms to create visual depth and interest even when nothing is in bloom.

Height Variation

Creating visual depth with layered plantings, including tall backdrop plants, mid-layer specimens, and ground-level borders.

Seasonal Interest

Ensuring something beautiful in every season, from spring bulbs to summer blooms to fall foliage and winter structure.
