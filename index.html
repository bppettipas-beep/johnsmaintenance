Our Story

Built on Trust, Shaped by Experience, Inspired by You

Based in Moncton, New Brunswick, John's Maintenance & Landscaping has been a trusted name in the community for over four decades. What began as a passion for horticulture has grown into a full-service landscaping operation known throughout the region for dependability, craftsmanship, and genuine care for each client's property.

We specialize in softscape projects, creating and maintaining the living elements of your landscape. From instant sod lawns and seeded turf to carefully designed garden beds and the structural beauty of retaining walls and brick walkways, every project is treated as if it were our own yard.
