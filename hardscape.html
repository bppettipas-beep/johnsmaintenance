Hardscape refers to the solid, structural elements of your outdoor space — the walls, walkways, steps, and patios that define how your property looks and functions. Done right, hardscape adds lasting value, solves practical problems like erosion and drainage, and creates outdoor spaces you'll actually use. With over 40 years of experience building in New Brunswick, we know how to construct hardscape that holds up through our freeze-thaw cycles and stands the test of time.

Retaining Walls

  • Site assessment & drainage planning
  • Natural stone or block construction
  • Engineered for slope & soil pressure
  • Creates usable flat areas on slopes

Brick Walkways

  • Custom layout & pattern design
  • Compacted gravel base for stability
  • Edging & polymeric sand finish
  • Paver, brick, and natural stone options

Steps & Entryways

  • Natural stone, brick & block options
  • Safe, code-compliant rise & run
  • Matching wall & border accents
  • Lasting first impressions for your home

Patios & Seating Areas

  • Paver, flagstone & natural stone
  • Proper base prep & drainage grading
  • Fire pit & seating wall add-ons
  • Designed for entertaining & relaxing

Why Choose Our Hardscape Services?

Built for New Brunswick's Climate

Freeze-thaw cycles are hard on poorly built hardscape. We use proper base depths, drainage planning, and quality materials so your investment doesn't shift, crack, or sink after the first winter.

Proper Base Preparation

Every project starts with the right foundation. We excavate to the correct depth and compact a granular base that won't shift through our freeze-thaw seasons.

Quality Materials Only

We source premium stone, brick, and block. Cheap materials fail in a few years. Quality materials mean your hardscape looks great and holds up for decades.

Attention to Detail

Clean lines, level surfaces, tight joints. The details are what separate a professional installation from a rushed one, and we take pride in every finish.

Owner-Supervised Work

John is personally involved in every hardscape project. You get the benefit of 40+ years of hands-on experience, not a crew working without oversight.
